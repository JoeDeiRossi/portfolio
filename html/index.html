About Me

My name is Joe Dei Rossi, and I am a developer with a passion for the front end and a wide range of experience. Prior to diving into web development, I earned my BS in Materials Engineering at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, then worked in the healthcare industry for a few years. I worked at a family-owned optometry office for a year before moving to Orange County, where I worked at an international medical device company as an analyst for two years. Although my degree didn't directly lend itself to these positions, my time at Cal Poly was invaluable. It might sound cheesy, but the most important thing I learned in college was how to learn, which is absolutely essential as a developer.

I've always been intrigued by coding and being able to create websites, applications, and games. The day after leaving my job as an analyst, I started a two week "Intro to HTML/CSS" course with LearningFuze, an accelerated web development program, followed by their "Intro to JavaScript" course, and I was immediately hooked! I decided to enroll in LearningFuze's full program, and before I knew it I had gone from building basic web pages to complex applications and games utilizing languages like JavaScript and PHP, as well as frameworks including React, jQuery, and others.

After finishing up at LearningFuze, it seemed fitting that I should kick off my new career in a new city, so I moved to Portland! I'm looking forward to exploring the city while expanding and improving my skills as a developer.
